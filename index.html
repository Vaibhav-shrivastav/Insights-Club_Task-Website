About



"Every hardship has a reason and every deprivation a purpose, if you look through eyes of insight."
We are beyond pleased to announce the establishment of ‘Insights Club', a club to reignite the sharp minds of our students.
Our goal is to create and distribute reports about the various newsworthy events, facts, ideas, and celebrated personalities .
We will hold intra-campus events where students irrespective of their branch and campus are welcome to participate.
We aim to work together to develop a positive informed environment for the constant growth of intellect.
